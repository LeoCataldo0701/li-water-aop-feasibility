# li-water-aop-feasibility
A chemical kinetic simulation and economic optimization framework evaluating Advanced Oxidation Process (AOP) feasibility for local PFAS groundwater remediation.

Language: Python

Solvers: SciPy Solve_ivp BDF method

Context: PFAS found at 1000x regulation concentrations in Suffolk County LI

Conclusions: Even modern-leaning AOPs are economically impractical to remove such tremendous amounts of PFAS from water sources; either water main extensions or large government funding to carry out remediation techniques are needed.
